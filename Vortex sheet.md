[[Velocity potential]]
$$\phi(x,z)=-\frac{1}{2\pi}\int_{a}^{b}\theta\gamma ds$$
[[Circulation]]
$$\gamma=u1-u2$$
$$\Gamma=\int \gamma ds$$
From [[Kutta-Joukowski theorem]]
$$L'=\rho_\infty V_\infty \Gamma$$
And due to the trailing edge conditions:
$$\gamma(TE)=V1-V2=0$$
