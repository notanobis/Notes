## Κόρος - Saturation
Βραχυκύκλωμα
$$Ib>Ib_{sat}=\frac{Ic_{sat}}{\beta}$$
$$Vi_{sat}=RbIb_{sat}+0.7$$
$$Vi>Vi_{sat}$$
$$V_{CE}\approx 0.2$$
VΒΕ works as a [[Diode]]
$$V_{BE}=0.7V$$
